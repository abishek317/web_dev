Movies - Ranked

Here are my top 3 movies of all time:

Interstellar

An amazingly written movie about space and time. Emotional. Draws a beautiful connect between 2 concepts: Love and Time

Shutter Island

A riveting thriller about an officer investigating a suspicious asylum facility. Has one of the greatest plot twists I have experienced

Memento

A very confusing, yet amazing movie about a man searching for his wife's killer. Well written, with a very satisfying end
